Setting The PC Up


Now that you have your PC built and put together. It is time for you to power it on. Once you power it on you will be taken to the bios.

Here is where you will put in your flash drive or disk installed with Windows or Linux and follow the set up instructions. Once the os is set up all you have to do now is initalize your storage devices in the BIOS and then you should be good to go.
